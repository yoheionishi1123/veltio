Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2025

Veltio ("we", "our" or "the Company") establishes this Privacy Policy ("Policy") regarding the handling of personal information of users of our web service "Veltio" ("the Service") as set out below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information in providing the Service.

  • Registration information such as email address, name, and company name
  • Access logs for the Service (IP address, browser information, pages visited, etc.)
  • Google OAuth credentials (access tokens, refresh tokens) obtained for GA4 integration
  • Content such as analysis notes and campaign logs entered by the user

2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes.

  • Providing, operating and improving the Service
  • Responding to user support inquiries and sending notifications
  • Preventing unauthorised use and ensuring security
  • Creating statistical data (in a form that does not identify individuals)

3. Provision to Third Parties

We do not provide users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases.

  • When the user has given consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect a person's life, body or property

4. Use of External Services

The Service uses the following external services.

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Used to retrieve access analysis data from the user's own site linked to the Service. Data obtained is processed through the GA4 Data API and is subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics (Veltio's own measurement): Used to analyse usage of the Service.

5. Use of Cookies

The Service uses cookies for session management. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features of the Service may not be available if you do so.

6. Management and Protection of Personal Information

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or falsification of personal information. Passwords are stored in hashed form, and authentication information is managed with encryption.

7. Disclosure, Correction and Deletion of Personal Information

Users may request disclosure, correction or deletion of their own personal information from us. Please contact us via the contact form.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may change this Policy as necessary. The revised Policy shall take effect from the time it is posted on this page.

9. Contact

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please use the contact form.
